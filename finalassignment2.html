Facts of Saskatchewan

Climate

More sunshine hits Saskatchewan than any other province of Canada Drought can occur due to low rates of precipitation Saskatchewan is one of the few areas that has active events of tornados.

Education

The First Nation family groups and early fur trading settlers were the first education that occurred on the prairies. All schools except one are English first language schools The Saskatchewan ministry of education is responsible for all the funding of primary and secondary schools.

Government

Saskatchewan has the government system as the other provinces of Canada The Saskatchewan New Democratic Party dominates the politics in Saskatchewan Scott Moe is the premier of Saskatchewan.
