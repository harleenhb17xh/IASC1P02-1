Saskatchewan Statistics

Saskatchewan is a province in western Canada. It has an area of 651,900 square kilometres, consists of many rivers and reservoirs, approximately 10 percent of consists of fresh water, and has about 100,000 lakes. Saskatchewan is neighboured by the Northwest Territories on the north, Alberta on the west, Manitoba on the east, and by the United States of America on the south. Saskatchewan's population was estimated to be approximately 1,174,462. In the southern prairie fragment of the province, there are residential areas, whereas the northern half of the province is mostly forested and not very populated. Saskatchewan’s largest city is Saskatoon, this is where almost half of the population resides, or Regina, the capital of the province.
